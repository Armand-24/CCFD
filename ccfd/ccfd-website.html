What we do

Full-spectrum logistics & compliance

We manage every step — from navigating government ministries and customs authorities to coordinating international freight and ensuring equipment reaches the people who need it.

Regulatory Authorisation

End-to-end management of Ministry of Health and ABREMA approval processes, including application preparation, follow-up, and documentation.

Exemptions & Duty Management

Handling of tax exemptions, airport fees, and customs clearance to ensure timely and cost-effective importation of equipment.

Supplier Sourcing

Identification, vetting, and selection of qualified international suppliers, including purchase order negotiation and management.

Import & Freight Coordination

International freight arrangement, cargo tracking, and coordination with customs brokers for smooth border clearance.

Last-Mile Delivery

In-country logistics, routing, and delivery of equipment directly to beneficiary facilities across any geography.

Installation Oversight

On-site supervision to ensure equipment is properly commissioned, tested, and fully operational before handover to facility staff.

Ultrasound Machine Importation & Hospital Delivery Programme

Ministry of Health Partner Initiative — Three Beneficiary Hospitals

Delivery & Installation

CCFD managed the complete lifecycle of importing four ultrasound machines with full accessories — securing official authorisations from the Ministry of Health and ABREMA, managing airport fees and customs exemptions, selecting the supplier, placing the order, coordinating international freight, clearing customs, and delivering the equipment to three beneficiary hospitals where it was installed and commissioned.

All logistical and administrative arrangements were handled internally by CCFD, resulting in fully operational, installed equipment delivered without delays or obstacles.

How we work

A sequenced approach to complex logistics

Every engagement follows a clear, proven sequence — eliminating surprises and keeping every stakeholder informed at each stage.

Regulatory Mapping

Identify all required authorisations, exemptions, and compliance requirements before any order is placed.

Authorisation & Clearance

Pursue Ministry, ABREMA, and customs approvals in parallel to minimise lead time.

Supplier Engagement

Delivery & Handover

Route equipment to each facility, oversee installation, and confirm full operational readiness before handover.
